# Code of conduct

Desk follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). Short version: be a person.

- Be welcoming to newcomers regardless of experience level.
- Disagree on ideas; never attack people.
- If you see behavior that crosses a line, contact the maintainer at alireza.sheikholmolouki@gmail.com.

The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, commits, PRs, and to ban contributors whose behavior is judged inappropriate, threatening, or harmful — at any point and without prior notice.
